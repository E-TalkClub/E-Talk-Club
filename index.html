Why We Started E-Talk Club

E-Talk Club was founded to create a friendly and supportive space for students to practice spoken English. Many young learners study English in school, but they may not have enough chances to use it in real conversations.

Our club helps bridge that gap by offering speaking practice, discussion activities, and peer support. Through our volunteer sessions, we hope to help students become more confident, expressive, and comfortable when communicating in English.

What We Do

We focus on practical, engaging, and supportive methods to help students thrive.
🗨

English Conversation Practice

We provide regular speaking practice to help students use English more naturally in real-life communication.

☆

Confidence Building

We encourage students to speak up, make mistakes, and improve through practice in a positive environment.

👥

Interactive Activities

Our sessions may include discussion topics, games, short speeches, Q&A practice, and presentation activities.

🤝

Volunteer Service

Club members contribute their time to support younger students and promote meaningful English learning.
