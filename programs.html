What We Do

Our programs focus on real communication, confidence building, and supportive peer learning.

English Conversation Practice

We provide regular speaking practice to help students use English more naturally in real-life communication.

Confidence Building

We encourage students to speak up, make mistakes, and improve through practice in a positive environment.

Interactive Activities

Our sessions may include discussion topics, games, short speeches, Q&A practice, and presentation activities.

Volunteer Service

Club members contribute their time to support younger students and promote meaningful English learning.

E-TALK CLUB

A student-led volunteer organization helping younger students build English speaking confidence through conversation and peer support.
