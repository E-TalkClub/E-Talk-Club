About Us

Learn about the story, mission, and people behind E-Talk Club. Our club is supported by student leaders, volunteer tutors, department members, and a school teacher advisor.

Our Story

Many young learners study English in school, but they may not have enough chances to use it in natural conversations. E-Talk Club was founded to help bridge that gap by creating a supportive environment where students can practice speaking without fear.

Through conversation practice, interactive activities, and volunteer guidance, we hope to help students become more confident, expressive, and comfortable when communicating in English.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support younger students in developing spoken English confidence through friendly peer-led learning. We believe that language learning is most meaningful when students feel encouraged, respected, and willing to try.
