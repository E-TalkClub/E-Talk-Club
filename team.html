Our Team

E-Talk Club is supported by student leaders, student tutors, department members, and a school teacher advisor.

Executive Team

Yiwen (Ivy) Chen

President

Ivy is the President and Founder of E-Talk Club. She is responsible for the club's overall planning, activity design, communication, and leadership. She founded E-Talk Club to create a supportive space where younger students can practice spoken English, build confidence, and enjoy learning through real conversations.

Student Feedback “Ivy is kind and supportive during practice. She helps students feel more comfortable speaking English, and her sessions are friendly and easy to follow.” — E-Talk Club Participant

Yifan (Kevin) Wu

Vice President

Kevin is the Vice President of E-Talk Club. He takes a leadership role in organizing club activities, coordinating volunteer work, and supporting younger students during English speaking sessions. With patience, responsibility, and strong teamwork, Kevin helps guide both volunteers and participants in a welcoming learning environment.

Student Feedback “Kevin is really patient and easy to talk to. He makes the practice feel relaxed, so I’m not afraid to make mistakes. After a few sessions, I felt more comfortable speaking English and answering questions.” — E-Talk Club Student

Student Tutors

Student Volunteer Tutors

Our student tutors are trained E-Talk Club volunteers who support younger students during English speaking sessions. They lead conversations, encourage participation, and help students build confidence in a friendly and comfortable environment.
